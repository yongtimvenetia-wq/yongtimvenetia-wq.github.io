Kinship's tele-vet platform connects 20K+ subscribers with veterinarians over chat. The catch: wrap-up time after each consult was eating into care capacity, and summary quality varied wildly.

We embedded a Claude 3 Haiku agent directly inside the Twilio Flex console — extracting talking points, suggesting relevant articles, and drafting the client-facing summary in real time. Every suggestion is upvote/downvote-able so the model gets sharper.
